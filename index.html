On this site we have made an evaluation of the University of Louisiana Lafayette's website. We have done so by using Jacob Nielsen's 10 usability heuristics. They are all listed in the menu to the left.

We are two people who have worked together. We started by analyzing the website individually and noting the problems we found. Then we discussed all the problems and went through the website again together.
